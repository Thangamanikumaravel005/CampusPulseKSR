About Our College

K.S.R. College of Engineering (KSRCE), Tiruchengode is an autonomous institution affiliated with Anna University. Established in 2001 by Dr. K. S. Rangasamy under the Aarthi Educational and Charitable Trust, the college is currently led by Chairman and Managing Trustee R. Srinivasan. KSRCE offers undergraduate, postgraduate, MBA, MCA and Ph.D. programmes and is accredited by NAAC with A++ Grade, ensuring excellence in technical education.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education and learning opportunities.
  • Promote innovation, creativity and research.
  • Encourage participation in academic and extracurricular activities.
  • Develop responsible professionals and future leaders.
  • Create a collaborative and student-friendly environment.
